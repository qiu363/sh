Welcome to ROMETTLE, your trusted complete solution provider of high-quality undercarriage components. Established in 1998 in Jining city, ROMETTLE has over 25 years of manufacturing experience with a workforce of 490+. Our core team has a decade of experience in R&D and production management.

At ROMETTLE, we understand the vital role of undercarriage components in equipment performance and durability. Our mission is to enhance your machinery’s efficiency and longevity through excellent undercarriage products and professional services.

Production Bases

4 production bases in Jining and Xuzhou, ROMETTLE provides complete production process of track rollers, carrier rollers, idlers, sprockets, track chains, track shoes and other undercarriage components.
With a total investment of USD700 Million, Romettle has upgraded its production bases by expanding the area to 85 acres, the production capacity is 50,000 sets Roller and 30,000 sets Track shoes, which including different models.

Meaning of ROMETTLE

"Romettle" combines "Rome," evoking the ancient city of Rome, and "Mettle," signifying courage and spirit, with a pronunciation similar to "Metal."
