Building a Competitive, Innovative and Sustainable Ecology

New energy vehicles in China have been developed rapidly from the initial introducing period into a scaled producing and rapid growth period. Moreover, new technologies with new business models such as internet, autonomous driving, car-sharing and O2O have impacted the development of new energy automobile industry significantly. The driving force of promoting new energy vehicle’s development, will transfer to new mode of a joint driven by the policy and the market.

To unify the market and fair competition environment, regardless of existing enterprises or new entrants which are crucial environment roles, it needs a consistent promotion jointly by the authorities, companies and the community as well. Therefore, the mission of developing EV industry lies on innovations of technologies and business models, it enables more new technologies could rapidly apply to the industry, keeping creative and active, aiming to a new growth point of consumption and investment. The key of realizing the sustainable development of the EV industry, is how to maintain a good relationship between the policy makers and the market, in order to achieve a successful transfer under a joint coordination of the government and the market.
